About Us

Welcome to ジャンケン pon!

We're just a bunch of curious minds (okay, maybe just one with too much caffeine) who had a simple question: “Why press buttons when your hands can do the talking?”

So we built something different — a classic game, powered by modern tech. This isn’t your average Rock-Paper-Scissors match. This is you vs. the machine, in a fast-paced battle of reflexes and recognition.

Using your webcam and a touch of AI magic, our game detects your hand gestures in real time — Rock, Paper, or Scissors — and instantly responds with the bot’s choice. No clicks. No delays. Just lift your hand, throw your move, and let the showdown begin.

It’s fun. It’s weirdly addictive. And trust us, it’s harder to beat a computer that never hesitates.

Behind the scenes, we’re blending computer vision and machine learning into a seamless game experience that feels natural, responsive, and just a little bit futuristic. Whether you're testing your reaction speed, settling an argument, or just trying to outwit a camera-powered opponent, we hope this brings a smile (and maybe a win streak) to your day.

So go ahead — pick your move.
But fair warning: the bot doesn’t blink... and it learns fast.

The Game

Paper

Smooth. Sneaky.
Deadlier than it looks and straight forward.
Covers rock with grace and elegance, but gets shredded by scissors.

Basically the ninja of the trio — soft, but deadly.

Rock

Solid. Unyielding.
The heavyweight champ of the hand world.
Crushes scissors, but gets smothered by paper.

Often chosen by people who want to look tough but secretly fear paper cuts.

Scissors

Sharp. Precise.
Most stylish out of the three.
Slices through paper like nobody’s business, but gets crushed by rock(ouch).

Often picked by people who live on the edge.
